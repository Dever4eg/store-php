Description

"MacBook" is a term used for a brand of Mac notebook computers that Apple started producing in 2006. The American multinational corporation created MacBook computers when it consolidated its PowerBook and iBook lines during its transition to Intel processor-based products.

Now equipped with seventh-generation Intel Core processors, MacBook is snappier than ever. From daily tasks like launching apps and opening files to more advanced computing, you can power through your day thanks to faster SSDs and Turbo Boost processing up to 3.6GHz.
